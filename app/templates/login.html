New York Center for Public History

Oral History App

Thanks to a generous grant from the Fulsome Foundation, NYCPH is collecting oral histories of people from the five boroughs of New York City. The goals of this project are to help build the historical record and to give people a chance to record and organize their own personal collections and share these with friends and family online. On this web app you can upload the interviews you record and access the interviews that have been uploaded by yourself and others. Although our intent is to make all interviews completely public and freely accessible, at present the interviews are password protected as we await Institutional Review Board (IRB) approval.

In the spirit of transparency and freedom of information, we provide all source code and instructions for setting up and using this tool on at GitHub/Springle3152/ohapp.
